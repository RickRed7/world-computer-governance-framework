# Architecture: World-Computer-Governance-Framework (WCGF)

## Overview
The WCGF is the master integration layer. It provides a sovereign, decentralized operating system for global state management. It combines the resource routing of `NME` and the process management of `Executive-Kernel`.

## Core Logic
### 1. Decentralized Executive (DeEx)
Instead of a single human process, the executive function is handled by a Multi-Signature DAO. Decisions require cryptographic proof of consensus rather than a single-point-of-failure "order."

### 2. Universal State Machine (USM)
A high-integrity ledger that records every change in global energy status, legislative updates, and resource allocation. All "System Calls" are transparent and immutable.

### 3. Sovereign Sharding
Nation-states are refactored into "Shards." Each shard maintains its own local rules and culture but adheres to the global "Interoperability Protocol" for trade, energy exchange, and conflict resolution.
